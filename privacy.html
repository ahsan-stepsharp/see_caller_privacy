Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

See Caller ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Contact Data: To provide caller identification services, the app processes your saved contacts. We normalize and hash phone numbers locally on your device before uploading them to our shared reverse-lookup index.

We collect your Email Address through Google Sign-In to create your account and manage your data deletion requests.

2. How We Use Data

  • Identification: Hashed contact data is used to match incoming calls with known names for all users.
  • Account Management: Your email is used to uniquely identify your contributions so you can delete them later.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or contact list with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only used within the See Caller ecosystem for the sole purpose of caller identification.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the "Right to be Forgotten." You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app settings or via our Data Deletion Portal.
